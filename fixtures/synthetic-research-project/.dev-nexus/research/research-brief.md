# Research Brief

## Topic

Synthetic baseline fixture for DevNexus-Research setup and paper workflow
checks.

## Research Question

Can a DevNexus project represent a small research-paper workflow with durable
artifacts, a minimal LaTeX manuscript, and no required external services?

## Scope

- Use synthetic material only.
- Exercise artifact path rendering, LaTeX readiness, and worker briefing.
- Avoid claims about real systems, empirical results, or external sources.

## Human Checkpoints

- The human researcher owns the research question, interpretation, authorship,
  and export decision.
- Agents may inspect the fixture and propose edits, but manuscript mutation
  still requires an explicit scope.
