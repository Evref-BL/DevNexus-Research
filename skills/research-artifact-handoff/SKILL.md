# Research Artifact Handoff

Use this skill at the end of a research work session, before delegating to
another agent, or when preparing a reproducible research state summary.

## Allowed Mutation Mode

Artifact mutation for handoff notes and inventories when authorized. Do not
change manuscript content, bibliography entries, claim support, or source
decisions while preparing a handoff.

## Artifact Inputs

- Research brief
- Source manifest
- Material passport when available
- Claim register
- Bibliography state
- LaTeX build report
- Review package and revision matrix when available

## Workflow

1. Inventory artifacts, their freshness, and their owner.
2. Summarize completed work, decisions, verification, and open blockers.
3. Distinguish evidence-backed facts, assumptions, and unresolved questions.
4. Name the next safe action and the human checkpoint required before mutation.
5. Include privacy, sharing, or venue-disclosure constraints when known.

## Artifact Outputs

- Handoff summary
- Artifact inventory
- Open blocker list
- Next safe action

## Source Support

Do not upgrade assumptions into facts during handoff. Link claims to source
manifest entries, bibliography keys, or claim-register records, and label
unverified material clearly.

## Human Checkpoints

The human researcher confirms what can be shared, what remains private, and
which unresolved decisions require human review before further work.
